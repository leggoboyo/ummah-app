# Minimal Store Listing Template

## App Name

Ummah App

## Short Description

Offline-first Muslim app with free prayer times, adhan alerts, qibla, and respectful multi-school guidance.

## Full Description

Ummah App is built for Muslims who want reliable essentials without accounts, ads in core worship features, or unnecessary data collection.

Free core features:

- offline prayer times calculated on-device
- local adhan notifications and quiet notification modes
- qibla bearing
- Hijri date summary
- fiqh profile defaults for Sunni and Shia users with respectful school selection

Optional paid modules:

- Quran Plus
- Hadith Plus
- citation-first AI Q&A over Quran and hadith
- scholar and article feed

Privacy approach:

- no account required for free core features
- prayer times and qibla work offline
- analytics are off by default
- any future AI key can be stored locally on-device in secure storage

Important note:

The app presents sourced views and references. It does not replace qualified scholars, especially for context-heavy or high-risk questions.
